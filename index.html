02
E-commerce SEO Keyword Research Content Optimization

Hola Keto

A niche keto e-commerce brand with great products but almost no organic search visibility. The challenge: compete against established food blogs and big retailers in a crowded space.

↑ 3.8% Avg. CTR improvement after meta rewrite
100% Product pages optimized buyer-intent keywords
↑ Organic impressions significant growth
12 Blog posts published topic cluster strategy
How I did it
01
Research

Used Ahrefs and Semrush to map buyer-intent keywords across the full funnel — from "keto diet plan" (informational) to "buy keto snacks online" (transactional) for every product category.

02
On-page overhaul

Rewrote every product page title, meta description, H1, and body copy. Added structured data for products and FAQs to capture rich snippets in search results.

03
Content strategy

Built a topic cluster of 12 blog posts around high-volume keto keywords, each internally linking to relevant product pages — creating a content flywheel that builds authority over time.
