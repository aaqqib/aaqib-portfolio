    B A Barry Group — babarry.com

    B A Barry is a Dubai-based FMCG and commodities trading company (DMCC free zone) supplying bulk sugar and cocoa powder to B2B buyers globally. Brought in to diagnose a traffic drop and build a long-term SEO foundation. Identified a February 2026 site outage (5xx errors) as the root cause of a 38–47% traffic collapse. Conducted a full GSC and GA4 analysis, audited 14 pages sitewide for weak or truncated title tags and meta descriptions, and delivered optimised copy for all 14 — client implemented immediately. Performed deep landing page audits for three core product pages (ICUMSA 45 Sugar, Cocoa Powder, Organic Sugar) — diagnosing intent mismatches, CTR killers, missing content sections, and structural issues. Delivered new titles, metas, H1s, and CMS-ready body copy across all three pages. Identified 113 crawlable 404 pages, missing robots.txt, duplicate canonicals, and asset file errors wasting crawl budget. Conducted a full keyword gap analysis, identified 30 net-new blog opportunities across 5 content clusters, and wrote 4 SEO blogs targeting high-intent commercial keywords. Result: Organic sessions grew 44% month-on-month (322 → 465), organic overtook Direct as the #1 traffic channel for the first time, and average search position improved from 16.4 to 7.7.
